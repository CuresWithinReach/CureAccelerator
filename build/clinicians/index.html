What can I do with CureAccelerator?

Contribute your knowledge of effective off-label use  to drive clinical care and research

CureAccelerator’s community is composed of like-minded people who are dedicated to creating effective treatments through repurposing.

Share
Share observations off-label use

Your field experience could be the basis of the next new treatment. Start a discussion and share your observations today.

Colleagues
Engage Your Clinical Colleagues

Help translate your pre-clinical findings into clinical trials. Ask clinicians are your institution to join, or connect with clinicians on CureAccelerator who are already passionate about repurposing research.

Post
Post repurposing research ready for testing

Do you have an idea for a repurposed treatment ready for a clinical trial? We want to know about it, and so does the CureAccelerator funding community. Create a Project Proposal Summary today.
